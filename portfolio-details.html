My Factory

Mobile application that provides decision makers with remote and real-time access to track the three key resources of the factory (HUMAN, PRODUCT, MACHINE), to enhance the efficiency and capabilities of manufacturers by providing graphs and statistics that describe the status of machines and productivity level in real-time. An alert system is also integrated into the My Factory application that allows you to react instantly to reduce damages and losses.
